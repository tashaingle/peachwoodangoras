Care Information

Angoras are wonderfully fluffy, but they do need regular grooming. Here’s a gentle starter guide.

Grooming
Regular brushing/trim schedule to prevent matting.
Diet
Unlimited hay, quality pellets, fresh water, safe greens.
Housing
Clean, dry, draft-free; space to hop and stretch.
Handling
Calm handling builds confidence and trust.
